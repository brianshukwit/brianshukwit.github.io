Brian Shukwit

Aspiring Novelist, Brian Shukwit: Imagination has always been one of my greatest tools. Escaping to far away worlds in an instant with just a thought. Creating places, friends, dangers, adventure and excitement all in the comfort of my own mind. The stories would build up so I just HAD to get them out – so I began writing them down. At an early age, they were not some genius material or prodigy writing but it was fun for me. So I kept doing it and doing it. Now in my 30’s I have had enough people tell me to put myself out there, so here I am. I write because I enjoy it. I write because the worlds I create need to escape. I don’t write for money or fame, I write so people can have a piece of my imagination to take with them. I hope you enjoy my works and I look forward to hearing your thoughts. Stay tuned for more imagination!
